Inside the suite

Every tool your floor team and back office need.

01

Cashless RFID Cards and Wristbands

Tap to play across games and attractions. Cards roam between your locations, building loyalty at every visit.

02

Point of Sale

Fast, configurable POS for admissions, game cards, retail, and parties. Works on counters, tablets, and mobile.

03

Inventory and Redemption

Track tickets, prizes, and stock live. Guests redeem at counters or self-service Klaimprize kiosks.

Party and Event Reservations

Online booking, packages, deposits, and day-of coordination for the busiest revenue line in your venue.

Self Service Kiosks

Guests buy and recharge cards themselves. Shorter queues, lower labor cost, higher average spend.

Mobile POS and Wireless Readers

Sell and recharge anywhere on the floor with handheld readers and mobile point of sale.

Digital Signage

Menus, promos, and leaderboards that update themselves from the same platform.
