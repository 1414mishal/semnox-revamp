Corporate social responsibility

Creating value, spurring progress.

Sustainable value for clients, partners, shareholders, employees, and the communities that host us. CSR at Semnox has been formal policy since December 2016.

Focus areas

Nine commitments.

Education

Vocational training and job placement support for differently abled individuals.

Hunger and Nutrition

Supporting mid-day meal initiatives in schools across the country.

Rural Development

Roads, drainage, and electricity access for underserved communities.

Environment

Conservation of natural resources and ecological protection.

National Relief

Contributions to the Prime Minister's National Relief Fund.

Technology Incubators

Funding innovation hubs inside approved academic institutions.

Heritage and Culture

Restoration projects and promotion of traditional arts.

Sports

Training support for rural, Olympic, and Paralympic athletes.

Armed Forces

Assistance for veterans and war widows.

Governance

The CSR committee is chaired at the very top: Iqbal Mohammad, Kiran Karanki, and Umesh Prabhu personally steer where the effort goes.
